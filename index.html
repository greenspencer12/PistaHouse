Our Story

A Journey of Tradition & Excellence

Since 1997, Pista House has been dedicated to honouring the essence of tradition through our food. What started as a small family endeavour has grown into a global culinary destination, known for preserving the true essence of Hyderabadi cuisine.

At Pista House, we are not just about serving food; we are about crafting experiences that stay with you. Our world-famous Haleem, recognized and savoured by food lovers worldwide, is a testament to our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Our success lies in the careful attention to detail in every dish, from our iconic Biryani to our extensive menu of traditional delicacies. Each recipe is a piece of our heritage, passed down through generations and lovingly prepared to ensure that every bite is a journey into the heart of Hyderabadi culinary tradition.

What We Serve

Signature Dishes

Experience the flavors that have made us legendary across the globe

Hyderabadi Haleem

Our world-famous Haleem, slow-cooked for hours with the finest ingredients, embodying centuries of culinary tradition. A perfect blend of wheat, barley, lentils, and meat, garnished with caramelized onions and fresh herbs.

World Famous

Zafrani Dum Biryani

Experience the authentic Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, layered with premium basmati rice, tender meat, and aromatic saffron. Slow-cooked in traditional earthen pots to seal in the flavors and create an unforgettable taste.

Chef's Special

Biscuits & Bakery

Indulge in our artisanal bakery selection featuring traditional Osmania biscuits, fresh naan, and a variety of authentic breads and pastries. Each item baked fresh daily using time-honored recipes.

Meet Our Leaders

The Visionaries Behind Pista House

Mohd Abdul Majeed

Founder

An enterprising businessman with vision, foresight and drive allied with excellent knowledge on food and customer's tastes, Mr. Majeed a restaurant Industry veteran holds more than 24 years of food service industry experience and has very closely observed the needs of customers. This inspired him to start his own entrepreneurial venture Pista House which is running successfully at multiple locations.

Mohd Abdul Mohsi

Director

Mr. Mohsi has a singular vision of serving delicious food and bring the utmost comfort, satisfaction, quality and price for the customers. He always dreamt of a concept that would elevate the perception of Indian cuisine to be on par with French, Mexican, Italian etc, a dream that will now be a reality with the success of Pista House UAE.

Mohd Mohddis Ali

CEO

Following in his fathers' footsteps, Mr. Mohddis role as an Industrialist has been integral to the extraordinary development of Pista House. With an education in business studies, his well rounded approach is a testament to the success of the family run business.
